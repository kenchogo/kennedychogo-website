About Kennedy Chogo LLC

Strategic protection, intelligence, and risk advisory services.

Kennedy Chogo LLC is a security consulting and advisory firm focused on helping organizations, executives, investors, and private clients identify risks, strengthen protection measures, and operate confidently in changing environments.


Our approach combines security consulting, executive protection advisory, investigations, intelligence research, and regional expertise to provide practical solutions for real-world challenges.


We believe effective security begins with awareness, preparation, and informed decision-making. Our mission is to help clients anticipate challenges before they become crises.
