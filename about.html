About the Firm

Kennedy Chogo LLC provides security consulting and advisory services informed by more than two decades of practical security experience across operational environments, executive protection support, investigations support, risk management, and regional advisory services.

The firm works with organizations, executives, investors, ministries, nonprofits, and private clients seeking practical guidance to identify risks, improve preparedness, strengthen protective measures, and support informed decision-making.
